# Proposal: Medium article writer in my style

## Why
User gives a topic; system analyzes my previous Medium articles, researches the topic with analytical data, and drafts a human-sounding Medium article in my style under roughly a 9 minute read.

## What Changes
- Create a focused full-stack proof of concept for `Medium article writer in my style`.
- Validate whether the idea should be handed to the larger engineering crew.
- Keep the first draft local-first, secret-safe, and deploy-aware.

## Impact
- Adds a draft project under the workspace-level `pocs/mediumarticlewriterinmystyle/` folder.
- Adds frontend, backend, database, infra, DevOps, and deployment documentation.
- Defines implementation requirements in `specs/`.
